Frequently Asked Questions

What’s a compliant airdrop?

It’s possible — and perhaps likely — that airdropping tokens to users should be considered a securities offering by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). As such, issuers would need to use a valid exemption or register these offerings with the SEC. Airdrops by Coinheart is the product that allows issuers to use exemptions in a seamless manner to compliantly distribute tokens to users.

Who can you airdrop to?

Depending on the issuer, Airdrops by Coinheart can reach over 4 million users across Coinheart, AngelList, and Product Hunt. The product also allows for targeting users through a variety of facets, including job title and location.

How do I avoid someone creating multiple accounts?

Airdrops by Coinheart allows issuers to require identity verification of users for them to receive tokens. This means that nobody can create multiple accounts and scam the issuer — users are limited to one distribution per person.
